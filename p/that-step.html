The step you don’t want to take…

22-12-09 // 19:59 DUBAI


That step
You know
I know
The bad breaks

That step that is so close
but seems so far

That step that’s always there
It does not leave

Until you take it
Until I take it

It stays
It waits

It even calls me
sometimes

That step I hear so clearly
That step that scares me
That always used to scare me

It still scares me
but less
and less
and less

The step is my friend
It wants to help me
So I shall let it

The step that used to scare me
That is the step I did not want to take


Inspiration
Start in close by David Whyte
