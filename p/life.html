For life goes not backwards...

22-11-27 // 07:27 DUBAI


Life, it does go up and down
Sometimes left and sometimes right

But always forwards, never backwards

And when it does
then you might ask

What does this mean?

Is this not somehow going backwards?
Or at least staying on the same spot?

Then I would say
to you my friend

Well im not sure
to be quite honest

What does it actually mean for you?
This loop in life that you are living?

Is it not also progress in some form?

You live and learn
and with each lesson

Its you who then gets to decide
Which lesson to learn
and also how often ...

With that my friend,
I do implore you

Choose wisely

For life
and hence your time
is finite

And time goes forwards
never backwards

It will run out
Regardless
of what you choose to do



Inspiration
On Children by Kahlil Gibran
