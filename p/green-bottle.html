Little green bottle

22-12-19 // 18:11 Somewhere in the sky above Athens


Little bottles. I used to not understand.
Why buy a small bottle if you can buy a big one.

What waste. Of money and of resources.
Why buy
But even more, why bother producing?

Now I look at that little green bottle
Perrier
I understand.

It’s not about the size.
It’s about feeling
It’s about lifestyle

It’s not about efficiency
It’s about convenience

It’s not what’s good for the planet
It’s what is good for business

But that green bottle, glistening in the sun.
It told a story
It represented the world

My world
And the world outside

It was a key
A message
Unlocking many truths

Some now and some I’m sure in the future

That little innocent green bottle
Glazing in the sun
1000 miles up in the sky



Inspired by a little green Perrier bottle on the flight home for Christmas (DXB to HAM)
