Inward bound…

2022-12-13 // 07:50 DUBAI


Inward is the destination that we seek but rarely travel
Inward bound or outward gone
We lose ourselves outside
It’s outside that is crowded
The noise drowns our inner voice
It’s easier to dance and move in unison
To go into a crowded club
To move not where you want to go
But to be pushed to where the mob is moving
To numb yourself
Intoxicated
Fake elated
Ear shuttering
What for?
For fun?
Is this what fun should feel like?
Should fun leave you with shame and with regret?
Should fun require recovery?
Should fun not uplift us?
Should it not energize us?

Inward bound
Dare to go and be inward bound
For outward gone is easy
It’s the safest dangerous way

Inspired by Bahareh
