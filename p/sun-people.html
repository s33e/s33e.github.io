22-12-16 // 15:55 DUBAI


Sun people have hearts that radiate
They shine with their voice

The tone, it’s light and care free
My tone is heavy
There is sadness in my heart

Though I am sitting at one of the most beautiful places
my heart is heavy
my soul is empty

I know the drugs that distract me
It’s food
It’s others
and it’s sports

While sports is the best amongst the bunch
the former two often succeed

How can I know but then not do?
Is the pain not great enough?
Is this what I enjoy?
Is this where I want to be?

Would I not be running from the pain that is so great?
I start and stop and start and stop.

I feel what I feel but I am quick to change
BUT
It’s me who always gets to decide his fate

And decide I will.
I want and will and can

Because MOMENTS
They change everything
Vibrations
What you feel is what you get
So start to feel so you shall get

Cause you is I and I is you
It’s always me im talking to

The up and down it is taxing me greatly
But simply think what I really want

Think and do and then you’ll get
Just think not do then you’ll regret
