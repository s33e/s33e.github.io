Morning Thoughts Dubai

22-12-10 // 06:44 DUBAI


What’s important for me?
What do I want to do?
When am I happiest?

I like to be with friends.
I love and feel most comfortable at home with my family.
I am abundant.
I feel free.
I can tackle things head on.
I am the best but I am also kind and loving and caring.
It suits me the most to be in control of my life. Just as it suits anyone.
Develop a stronger bias for action.
Think - Think - Act.
It’s like boxing.
Make it into a dance
Enjoy.

I will finish my deck and get feedback.
I already know what the necessary steps are.

I will take them one by one.
It’s like speaking a new language.
You only learn by speaking.
Thinking only is mute.
Thought plus action leads to results.
The results can then be evaluated and iterated accordingly.
Think - Think - Do
Think - Think - Do
Just do it
But
Think first
And think second
But then act.

Always beware of thinking.
Control it.
Limited so that the path to action is clear.
The mind is mighty
and vast.
And it is easy to get absorbed.
To spend all of your time there.
To think about acting at the cost of acting.
Think timing.
Think action.
Act.
That’s all it is.
Not more, not less.
